All about Capybaras

Capybara's are the largest rodents in the world. These rodents can be found in South America. They can range from 77-150 pounds and can reach up to 25 inches tall and almost 4 and half feet long. They are great swimmers, as they mainly inhabit habitats near bodies of water. They have webbed feet perfect for swimming.

Where they can be found:

  1. Panama
  2. Colombia
  3. Venezuela
  4. Guyana
  5. Peru
  6. Brazil
  7. Paraguay
  8. Argentina
  9. Uruguay
